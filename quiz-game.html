Instructions

  • Click on the Start Quiz button at the top/left side.

  • After clicking a custom panel will appear. After reading the questionnaire carefully, click on the option below which you think is correct. Once clicked, the change is irreversible, so be careful.


    If the answer clicked by you is yes then it will be shown in green color and otherwise it will be shown in red (as seen in the above image).

  • After choosing the answer Show Answer button will be visible. It contains answer key and short explanation of answer key.

  • After choosing the answer, click on the Next button below to get the next question.

  • After completing the question, click on the Finish button below to see the score.

  • Click the Return To Homegame will finished.
